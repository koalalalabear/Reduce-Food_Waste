Banana: 2-7 days at room temperature / 1-2 weeks if refrigerated.
Beetroot: 1-2 weeks in the refrigerator.
Bell pepper: 1-2 weeks in the refrigerator.
Cabbage: 2-3 weeks in the refrigerator.
Carrot: 2-4 weeks in the refrigerator.
Cauliflower: 1-2 weeks in the refrigerator.
Corn: 1-2 days if not refrigerated / up to 3-5 days if refrigerated.
Cucumber: 1 week in the refrigerator.
Eggplant: 1 week in the refrigerator.
Garlic: 3-5 months if stored in a cool, dark, and dry place.
Ginger: 2-3 weeks if stored in the refrigerator.
Grapes: 1-2 weeks in the refrigerator.
Kiwi: 1-2 weeks if stored at room temperature, 2-3 weeks if refrigerated.
Lemon: 2-4 weeks at room temperature or longer if refrigerated.
Lettuce: 1 week in the refrigerator.
Mango: 1-2 weeks at room temperature, can be refrigerated for longer storage.
Onion: 2-3 months if stored in a cool, dry, and well-ventilated place.
Orange: 1-2 weeks at room temperature, longer if refrigerated.
Pear: 1-3 days at room temperature, 3-5 days if refrigerated.
Pineapple: 2-3 days at room temperature, 3-5 days if refrigerated.
Potato: 2-3 months if stored in a cool, dark, and dry place.
Peas: 2-3 days if not refrigerated, up to a week if refrigerated.
Tomato: 1 week at room temperature, 1-2 weeks if slightly underripe and left at room temperature, or 1-2 weeks if refrigerated.
Watermelon: 1 week at room temperature, or 2-3 weeks if refrigerated.
